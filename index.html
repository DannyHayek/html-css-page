Wok and Roll

The Best Japanese Restaurant in Town!

The Wok and Roll is a Japanese restaurant that serves traditional Japanese dishes, while also having a diverse fusion menu that takes dishes and cuisines from all over the world and combines them with traditional and contemporary Japanese dishes to create marvelous eating experiences.

Many creative plates and platters exist at this restaurant, and their extensive and varied menu has become a staple in downtown Astera, with many making trips to the New World to visit it and sample some of its exquisite foods and combinations.
