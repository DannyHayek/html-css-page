Information About the Wok and Roll

  • The restaurant opened in 1956, under the watchful and experienced eye and tutelage of the wonder Hidetaka Miyazaki. It has since been operating at a high level non-stop with only two temporary periods of closure for renovations.

  • Over the years, several head chefs of particular renown have manned the helm in the kitchen.

  • Most recently, Bob Franks, an up and coming world class chef, worked as our head chef for two years, greatly elevating the level of our cuisine and bringing in a slew of new dishes that revolutionized taste.

  • Our fusion cuisine has become a major point of attraction, bringing people from all around the world to come and sample of our unique food. While many competitors have risen, none have stood tall enough to challenge the Wok and Roll in its boundless pursuit for perfect taste.
